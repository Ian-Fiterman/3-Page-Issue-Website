Prevent Online Predators: An Informational Hub for Parents

Our Mission

Prevent Online Predators is dedicated to protecting children from the dangers of interacting with strangers online. Our mission is to raise awareness of the issue, educate parents through first-hand accounts of grooming, and provide informational resources to empower our communities to keep children safe online.

Important Statistics
  • In 2023, the CyberTipline received 36,210,368 reports of suspected child sexual exploitation.
  • Over 500,000 predators are active online every day.
  • 1 in 8 children has met someone face-to-face after first getting to know them online
  • 70% of children aged 14 to 17 have seen sexual images online.
  • 1 in 5 children aged 10 to 17 has been solicited sexually online.
  • In 50% to 70% of online child sexual abuse cases, the abuser is someone who is known to the child.
  • Some 80% of children in 25 countries report feeling in danger of sexual abuse or exploitation online.
  • Online grooming crimes have risen 82% in the last 5 years.
  • 73% of online grooming crimes involved Snapchat and Meta.
  • Where the gender was known, 83% of online grooming offences were against girls.
